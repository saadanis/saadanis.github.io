The Vexillology App

The iOS app for all things vexillology. The app features a list of all national flags of the world, which you can filter and group according to region, date founded, ratio, and much more. You can view details - a summary, design, origin, symbolism, and variants, etc. You can choose your favorite flags, or make custom lists. You can test your flag knowledge by playing quizzes. You can also learn about the principles of flag design, best practices, and the nomenclature and glossary of vexillology. I may or may not add the ability to create your own flags and generate wallpapers for any country using the flag.

 

TCAConcept

Concept for a routine creation app for iOS. Create routines for your life using TCAConcept. Routines can be started automatically at a given time with a list of tasks within it, each having its own time. The routine will continue and automatically (or manually) check off each task when its time has elapsed. Routines can be scheduled according to needs: weekly, daily, or at custom intervals.
